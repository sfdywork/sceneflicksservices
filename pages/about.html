Scene Flicks Company Profile

Revolutionizing multimedia, creativity, and events in Kenya and beyond.

Introduction

Scene Flicks is a dynamic and innovative company based in Kenya, East Africa, offering a wide range of creative and technical services to clients globally. Founded by the visionary entrepreneur and DJ, Domy Yeng, Scene Flicks has quickly established itself as a leader in the multimedia and event services industry. With a dedicated team of over 20 professionals working remotely, Scene Flicks combines talent, technology, and creativity to deliver exceptional results for every project.

Mission Statement

Our mission at Scene Flicks is to provide top-tier multimedia and event services that exceed our clients' expectations. We are committed to innovation, creativity, and excellence, ensuring that every project we undertake is executed to the highest standards. Our goal is to become a global leader in multimedia solutions, driven by a passion for quality and a commitment to client satisfaction.

Vision

Our vision is to revolutionize the multimedia and event services industry by harnessing the power of technology and creativity. We aspire to create unforgettable experiences and compelling visual content that resonate with audiences worldwide. At Scene Flicks, we believe in pushing the boundaries of what's possible, continually striving to innovate and inspire.

Core Values

  • Excellence: We are dedicated to delivering the highest quality services and products to our clients.
  • Innovation: We embrace new technologies and creative ideas to stay ahead in the industry.
  • Integrity: We conduct our business with honesty and transparency, building trust with our clients and partners.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of teamwork and collaboration, both within our company and with our clients.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do, and we are committed to exceeding their expectations.
